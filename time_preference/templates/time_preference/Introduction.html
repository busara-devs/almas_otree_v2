On the following screens you will find a series of four questions. In each question, you will be asked to make decisions involving payments over time. Each row will feature a series of options. Each option consists of a sooner payment and a later payment. There is a trade-off between the sooner payment and the later payment across the options. As the sooner payment goes down, the later payment goes up. Please choose the option you prefer. There are no right or wrong answers.

After you make all your choices, the computer will randomly select one of the questions. Your earnings from this part of the experiment will be your choice for the question selected by the computer.
