Instructions

You are now matched with two other players. You have each received KSH 75. You must now decide how much of your KSH 75 to put into a shared fund. The other players will simultaneously decide how much to put into the shared fund. Each KSH put into the fund is multiplied by 2 and shared equally between the three of you. Each KSH you do not put into the fund you get to keep for sure.

If you contribute KSH 50, and both other participants contribute KSH 50, the fund will increase to KSH 300 KSH and you all get back KSH 100.

For your convenience, these instructions will remain available to you on all subsequent screens of this study.
